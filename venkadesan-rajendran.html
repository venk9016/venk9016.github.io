About

Clinician. Scholar. Leader.

Venkadesan Rajendran is a registered physiotherapist working in the Medicine and Acute Stroke Unit at Health Sciences North in Sudbury, Ontario. He holds a PhD in Rehabilitation Science, a Master of Education in Leadership and Learning, and certificates in Medical Acupuncture from McMaster University and Stroke Rehabilitation from the University of Alberta.

As an Assistant Professor at Northern Ontario School of Medicine University, he teaches in the Clinical Sciences Division and works closely with health professional students from physiotherapy, medicine, nursing, and related disciplines. His research covers stroke rehabilitation, geriatric care, exercise in oncology, nursing research, patient experience, and acupuncture safety.

He served as President of the Northern District of Ontario Physiotherapy Association, with his term ending in 2024, and continues in his role as Education Coordinator for the Northern Division of the Ontario Physiotherapy Association.

Expertise

Research Focus Areas

Work spanning stroke rehabilitation, geriatric care, oncology, nursing research, patient experience, and acupuncture. All published in internationally indexed journals.

🧠

Stroke Rehabilitation

His work in this area looks at mirror therapy, motor imagery, and other rehabilitation approaches that help stroke patients regain their physical function and manage daily activities.

🏥

Geriatric Physiotherapy

This research looks at what happens when physiotherapy is provided on weekends for older patients in hospital. The goal is to understand whether additional therapy days reduce disability and keep patients moving during their stay.

🎗️

Oncology & Exercise

Working alongside colleagues, he developed clinical practice guidelines for physiotherapy in patients with multiple myeloma. These guidelines were reviewed and endorsed by the Canadian Physiotherapy Association.

📍

Acupuncture in PT Practice

He has published recommendations to help physiotherapists across Canada understand and manage the risks that can arise when acupuncture is used as part of treatment.

🤝

Patient Experience & Satisfaction

Designing and evaluating studies that capture what patients genuinely experience during and after care. This includes work on validated patient satisfaction instruments, Patient Oriented Discharge Summaries, and translating findings into practical improvements for care teams.

👂

Pediatric Rehabilitation

This earlier body of work examined balance, movement, and quality of life in children with hearing impairment, and tested whether targeted exercise could make a meaningful difference for these children.

📈

Quality Improvement

Leading QI initiatives using evidence based frameworks to enhance patient safety, optimize care pathways, and improve stroke patient outcomes and patient experience.

⚙️

Lean Six Sigma

Applying Lean Six Sigma methodologies to streamline rehabilitation service delivery, reduce waste, and advance process improvement in hospital based physiotherapy.

🎯

Advanced Practice Physiotherapy Competencies

Currently working on developing core competencies for advanced practice physiotherapists in stroke care, establishing a framework to guide role definition, professional standards, and training requirements for this emerging scope of practice in Canada.

Quality Improvement Consultation

From the first idea to the published result.

Dr. Rajendran offers quality improvement consultation to health professionals across disciplines, physiotherapy, nursing, medicine, allied health, and beyond. Whether you are a clinician with a care question, a nurse researcher developing a study, or a team preparing a grant proposal, he provides structured support from the first conversation to the final published paper.

What is included

The full project cycle covered

01
📋

Grant Proposal and Project Development

Dr. Rajendran has expertise preparing competitive grant applications and translating clinical observations into fundable, well structured proposals. This includes defining aims and objectives, selecting appropriate methodology, identifying stakeholders, writing the proposal narrative, and preparing budgets and timelines for submission.

02
⚙️

Project Design and Ethics Preparation

Support in designing your QI project using recognized frameworks such as the IHI Model for Improvement or PDSA cycles. Guidance through ethics and research board submissions where required.

03
📊

Data Collection and Analysis

Working through what data you need, how to collect it cleanly, and how to make sense of it. Statistical analysis, interpretation of results, and clear presentation of findings for clinical and academic audiences.

04
🔄

Implementation and Evaluation Support

Guidance on putting your intervention into practice, tracking progress, and evaluating outcomes. Support in adapting the project as you go, and preparing a clear picture of what worked and what did not.

05
✍️

Report Writing and Knowledge Translation

Turning your project findings into a clear, well structured written report for internal use, hospital leadership, or conference presentations. Help with plain language summaries for broader audiences.

06
📰

Publication Support

Support in preparing and submitting your work to peer reviewed journals. This includes manuscript structuring, journal selection, responding to reviewer feedback, and navigating the publication process from submission to acceptance.

07
🤲

Patient Experience and Satisfaction Research

Helping teams design and run studies that measure what patients actually experience during and after their care. This includes selecting validated patient experience and satisfaction instruments, designing surveys, analyzing results, and translating findings into actionable recommendations.

08
📄

Patient Oriented Discharge Summary (PODS)

Support in developing, implementing, and evaluating Patient Oriented Discharge Summary initiatives. Dr. Rajendran can help teams design PODS quality improvement projects, measure uptake and patient understanding, and prepare findings for publication or internal reporting.

09
💉

Nursing Research

Practical support for nurses and nursing teams developing clinical questions, designing studies, collecting and interpreting data, and preparing manuscripts for nursing and interdisciplinary journals.

Who this is for

Health professionals ready to improve care

This service is for health professionals across disciplines, nurses, physiotherapists, physicians, occupational therapists, respiratory therapists, social workers, dietitians, hospital managers, and interdisciplinary teams, who want to address a quality problem but need support with the research and publication side of things.

It is also well suited to clinicians who have already completed a project and want help turning their findings into a written report, internal document, or peer reviewed publication. Nursing research projects are particularly welcome. Geography is not a barrier, consultation is available remotely across Canada.
